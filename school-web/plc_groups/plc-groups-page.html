Pre-Cal

Throughout this semester, this course will prepare students for Calculus. Students will systematically work with functions and their multiple representations. The study of Precalculus deepens students’ mathematical understanding and fluency with Algebra and Trigonometry and extends their ability to make connections and apply concepts and procedures at higher levels.
